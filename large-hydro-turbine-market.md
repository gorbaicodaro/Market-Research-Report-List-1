<p><p>The large hydro turbine market encompasses various turbine types, each suited for specific operational conditions. Francis turbines are versatile and commonly used for medium head applications, providing efficient energy conversion. Kaplan turbines excel in low head environments due to their adjustable blades, optimizing efficiency. Pelton turbines are ideal for high head applications, utilizing kinetic energy from water jets for power generation. Other market types may include bulb and crossflow turbines, designed for niche applications, enhancing overall market diversity and efficiency.</p></p>
