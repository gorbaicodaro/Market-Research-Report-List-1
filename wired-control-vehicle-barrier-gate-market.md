<p><strong>The Wired Control Vehicle Barrier Gate Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Straight</li><li>Crank</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Wired control vehicle barrier gates are primarily categorized into two types: straight and crank models. Straight barriers feature a linear design that allows for rapid opening and closing, ideal for high-traffic areas. Crank barriers, on the other hand, utilize a manual or mechanical crank system for operation, providing more controlled access in settings where speed is less critical. Both types ensure secure vehicle entry and exit but cater to different operational needs and traffic management preferences.</p></p>
