<p><p>The Nuclear Air Filters market comprises two main types: portable and stationary filters. Portable nuclear air filters are designed for mobility and temporary use, often utilized in emergency situations or in areas with fluctuating contamination levels. They are compact and can be easily transported. Stationary nuclear air filters, on the other hand, are fixed systems installed in facilities such as nuclear power plants or research labs. They provide continuous air purification, ensuring a stable environment by removing airborne radioactive particles.</p></p>
