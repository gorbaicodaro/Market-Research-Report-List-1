<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Actuator Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electric</li><li>Electromechanical</li><li>Electrohydraulic</li><li>Electrohydrostatic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Actuator systems can be categorized into four primary types. Electric actuators convert electrical energy into motion, offering precision and reliability, commonly used in automation. Electromechanical actuators combine electrical and mechanical processes to provide efficient motion control in various applications. Electrohydraulic actuators use hydraulic fluid to transmit power, ideal for tasks requiring high force output, particularly in heavy machinery. Lastly, electrohydrostatic actuators integrate hydraulic systems with electric control, providing compact and efficient solutions for demanding environments.</p></p>
