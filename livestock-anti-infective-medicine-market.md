<p><p>The Livestock Anti-Infective Medicine Market comprises two primary types: external use and internal use medicines. External use medicines include topical treatments such as ointments, sprays, or disinfectants that target infections on the skin or wounds. Internal use medicines encompass oral medications, injectables, and other formulations administered to treat systemic infections affecting internal organs. Both types play a crucial role in maintaining animal health, preventing disease outbreaks, and ensuring the safety of livestock products in the food supply chain.</p></p>
