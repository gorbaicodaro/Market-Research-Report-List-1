<p><p>The conductive silver powder market is divided into two main types: flake silver powder and spherical silver powder. Flake silver powder features a flat, thin structure, enhancing its surface area and making it suitable for applications in printed electronics and conductive inks. Conversely, spherical silver powder offers a more uniform shape, providing better flow properties and packing density, making it ideal for paste applications and various electronic components. Both types play crucial roles in diverse industries, including electronics and photovoltaics.</p></p>
