<p><p>The dental anaesthetics market is primarily segmented into several key types: Lidocaine, Mepivacaine, Prilocaine, Articaine, and Others. Lidocaine is widely used for its rapid onset and effectiveness in pain control. Mepivacaine offers longer duration with less vasodilation, making it suitable for specific procedures. Prilocaine is known for its lower toxicity and effective pain management. Articaine, with its unique ester-amide structure, provides excellent diffusion and analgesia. The "Others" category encompasses less common agents or new formulations emerging in the market.</p></p>
