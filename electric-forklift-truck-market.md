Class 1 trucks are stand-up forklifts designed for indoor use, emphasizing maneuverability. Class 2 trucks, known as powered walkies or pallet trucks, are ideal for moving pallets short distances. Class 3 trucks consist of stringer and sit-down models, focusing on versatility in tight spaces. Class 4 and 5 trucks are more robust, suited for outdoor tasks and featuring advanced stability and lifting capacities.</p></p>
