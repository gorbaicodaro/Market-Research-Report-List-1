<p><strong>The Spin Bikes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Magnetic Resistance</li><li>Felt Pad Resistance</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The spin bike market is primarily segmented into magnetic resistance and felt pad resistance types. Magnetic resistance bikes utilize magnets to create resistance, providing smooth, quiet operation and requiring less maintenance. They offer a wide range of resistance levels, making them suitable for various fitness levels. On the other hand, felt pad resistance bikes use a friction system, where a felt pad presses against the flywheel to adjust resistance. While generally more affordable, they may require more maintenance and can be noisier during use.</p></p>
