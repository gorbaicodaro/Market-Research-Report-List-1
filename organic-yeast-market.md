<p><p>The organic yeast market is segmented into three main types: organic fresh yeast, organic dry yeast, and organic yeast extracts. Organic fresh yeast is typically used in baking for its leavening properties and must be refrigerated. Organic dry yeast has a longer shelf life and is convenient for home bakers and industrial applications. Organic yeast extracts are derived from yeast cells and serve as flavor enhancers or nutritional supplements in food products. Each type caters to different consumer needs and applications.</p></p>
