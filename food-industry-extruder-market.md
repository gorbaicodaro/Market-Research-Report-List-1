<p><strong>The Food Industry Extruder Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Screw</li><li>Twin Screw</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The food industry extruder market is primarily categorized into two types: single screw and twin screw extruders. Single screw extruders are simpler in design and are typically used for processing materials with a lower viscosity, making them suitable for producing snacks and cereals. Twin screw extruders, on the other hand, feature two intermeshing screws that allow for more complex processes, enabling better mixing, cooking, and customizing of food products such as pasta, pet food, and textured proteins.</p></p>
