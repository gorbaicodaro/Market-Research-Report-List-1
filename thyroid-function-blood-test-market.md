<p><p>The thyroid function blood test market includes several key types, assessing various thyroid hormones. Thyroid-Stimulating Hormone (TSH) is the primary regulator of thyroid hormone production. Thyroxine (T4) measures the main hormone produced by the thyroid gland, while Triiodothyronine (T3) assesses the more active form of the hormone. Thyroglobulin (Tg) serves as a tumor marker in certain thyroid cancers. These tests are essential for diagnosing thyroid disorders and monitoring treatment efficacy, driving market demand globally.</p></p>
