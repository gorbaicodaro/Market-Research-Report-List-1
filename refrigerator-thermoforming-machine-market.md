<p><ul><li>Single Station</li><li>Multi Station</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The refrigerator thermoforming machine market comprises two primary types: single station and multi-station machines. Single station machines operate one mold at a time, allowing for simpler operations and lower initial costs, suitable for smaller production needs. In contrast, multi-station machines can handle multiple molds simultaneously, enhancing efficiency and output, making them ideal for larger-scale production. The choice between these types depends on production volume requirements, cost considerations, and operational efficiency desired by manufacturers in the refrigeration sector.</p></p>
