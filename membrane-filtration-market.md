<p><ul><li>Reverse Osmosis Membranes (RO)</li><li>Ultrafiltration Membranes (UF)</li><li>Microfiltration Membranes (MF)</li><li>Nano-Filtration Membranes (NF)</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The membrane filtration market comprises various technologies, including Reverse Osmosis (RO), which effectively removes dissolved solids and contaminants through a semi-permeable membrane. Ultrafiltration (UF) membranes separate larger particles, colloids, and bacteria, while Microfiltration (MF) focuses on even larger particles and suspended solids. Nano-Filtration (NF) lies between ultrafiltration and reverse osmosis, targeting divalent ions and small molecules. Other market segments may include hybrid systems and specialized membranes designed for specific applications, enhancing versatility across industries such as water treatment, food processing, and pharmaceuticals.</p></p>
