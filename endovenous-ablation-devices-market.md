<p><p>The Endovenous Ablation Devices market comprises three main types: EVLT devices, RFA devices, and NTNT devices. EVLT (Endovenous Laser Treatment) utilizes laser energy to close varicose veins. RFA (Radiofrequency Ablation) employs radiofrequency energy for thermal ablation of vein tissue. NTNT (Non-Thermal Non-Tumescent) devices utilize methods like mechanical manipulation to treat veins without thermal energy, reducing patient discomfort. Each device type offers unique benefits, catering to various patient needs and preferences in venous disease treatment.</p></p>
