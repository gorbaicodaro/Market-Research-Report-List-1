<p><p>The Paste PVC resin market can be categorized into two main types: homogeneous and heterogeneous. Homogeneous PVC resin is characterized by a uniform structure, leading to consistent properties and performance across applications, often used in high-quality products. In contrast, heterogeneous PVC resin contains distinct phases or particles, resulting in varied characteristics, making it suitable for applications requiring specific flexibility or durability. Both types cater to diverse industrial needs, influencing their market dynamics and pricing strategies.</p></p>
