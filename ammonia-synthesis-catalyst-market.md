<p><p>The ammonia synthesis catalyst market is primarily categorized into three types: magnetite-based, iron-based, and others. Magnetite-based catalysts are favored for their high efficiency and stability at elevated temperatures, playing a crucial role in ammonia production via the Haber-Bosch process. Iron-based catalysts, while less efficient than magnetite, are cost-effective and widely used. The "others" category includes alternatives like ruthenium and cobalt catalysts, which are utilized in specific applications or under experimental conditions, catering to niche market demands.</p></p>
