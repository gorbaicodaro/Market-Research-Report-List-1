<p><strong>The Electric Vehicle Relays Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Plug-in Relay</li><li>PCB Relay</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Electric Vehicle Relays market includes two main types: plug-in relays and PCB relays. Plug-in relays are modular components that can be easily inserted into sockets, allowing for flexible configurations and maintenance. They are commonly used in high-power applications within electric vehicles. PCB relays, on the other hand, are soldered directly onto printed circuit boards, offering compactness and reliability for smaller applications. Both types are crucial for managing electrical loads and ensuring the efficient operation of electric vehicle systems.</p></p>
