High crosslinking density epoxy vinyl ester provides superior mechanical strength. Flexible epoxy vinyl ester maintains elasticity, suitable for flexible components. PU modified epoxy vinyl ester blends the advantages of polyurethane for improved toughness and adhesion, catering to diverse manufacturing needs.</p></p>
