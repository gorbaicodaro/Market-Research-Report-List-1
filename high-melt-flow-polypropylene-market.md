<p><p>The high melt flow polypropylene (PP) market is primarily segmented into two types: homopolymer and copolymer. Homopolymer PP is characterized by its high stiffness and low impact resistance, making it suitable for applications like packaging and automotive components. In contrast, copolymer PP combines properties of both polypropylene and polyethylene, offering enhanced flexibility and toughness. This versatility allows copolymers to be used in diverse applications such as consumer goods and industrial uses, catering to varying performance requirements.</p></p>
