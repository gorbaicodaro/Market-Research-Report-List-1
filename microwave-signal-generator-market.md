<p><p>The microwave signal generator market is divided into two main types: low frequency and high frequency. Low frequency microwave signal generators typically operate within the frequency range of a few MHz to several GHz, ideal for applications like testing and measurement in communications. In contrast, high frequency generators operate at GHz to THz ranges, catering to advanced applications in radar, telecommunications, and satellite communications. The choice between low and high frequency depends on the specific application requirements and technological advancements.</p></p>
