<p><p>Insect Growth Regulator (IGR) bait market comprises three main types: Chitin Synthesis Inhibitors, which disrupt the development of the exoskeleton in insects, preventing their maturation; Juvenile Hormone Analogs & Mimics, which simulate juvenile hormones to interfere with growth and reproduction, effectively stunting pest populations; and Anti-Juvenile Hormone Agents, which disrupt the normal hormonal balance, leading to premature development or sterility. Together, these IGRs target pest lifecycle stages, enhancing pest control strategies in various environments.</p></p>
